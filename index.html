Game Description

Defend your base against relentless waves of enemies in this fast-paced tower defense shooter!

  • Start with 100 health - lose 25 health each time an enemy breaches your defenses
  • Begin with 50 bullets - manage your ammo wisely
  • Earn 3 coins for every enemy defeated
  • Use coins to upgrade your arsenal or buy more ammo
