About Our Studio

Likha Studios is a small indie game development team dedicated to creating unique and engaging gaming experiences. Our focus is on innovation, creativity, and player satisfaction.

Our Vision

To create memorable gaming experiences that bring joy and excitement to players worldwide.

Our Mission

To develop innovative games that push creative boundaries while maintaining high quality and player engagement.

Our Achievements

Successfully launched Pong Clash! on itch.io, gaining positive feedback from the gaming community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What games do you develop? ▶

We develop various types of games, currently focusing on arcade-style games with modern twists. Our first release is Pong Clash!

How can I play your games? ▶

Our games are available on itch.io. You can find Pong Clash! and future releases on our itch.io page.

Do you accept game ideas from the community? ▶

Yes! We welcome suggestions and feedback from our community. Feel free to reach out through our contact form.

How can I join your team? ▶

We occasionally look for new talent. Follow our social media for job openings and opportunities.
